## 💡 What is Recharts?

- Recharts is a composable charting library for React that renders charts with SVG and leverages D3-inspired utilities under the hood.
- Provides a declarative, component-based API—build charts by composing pieces like LineChart, BarChart, AreaChart, PieChart, ScatterChart, RadarChart, RadialBarChart, ComposedChart, XAxis, YAxis, CartesianGrid, Tooltip, Legend, Brush, and ReferenceLine.
- Accepts simple data (arrays of objects) via dataKey props; integrates naturally with React state, props, and hooks.
- Includes responsive containers, animations, and built-in interactivity (tooltips, legends, brushing, highlighting).
- Highly customizable: override renderers, supply custom shapes/labels, or use render callbacks to tailor visuals.
- Ships with TypeScript definitions and solid documentation; open-source under the MIT license.
- Ideal for dashboards and reports with small to medium datasets; for millions of points, consider canvas/WebGL-based alternatives.
